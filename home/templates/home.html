Aya Hair Salon

Aya's Hair Salon is a local small business run by Aya Suleiman. She is an experienced hairstylist who holds many certifications and qualifications in hairstyling. Aya's creativity and flexibility is what sets her out of the crowd as she is able to deliver exaclty what the client wants


Aya accomodates the needs of women who wear the hijab. All of Aya's sessions will be in a private room with only her and the clients.
